Refinishing

 

 

Pine Cliff Woodworks does resurfacing and refinishing! So, your board has a rough surface or an unattractive stain needed to be removed? No sweat, just like that, we can make any old surface look brand new. All Pine Cliff customers receive an annual resurfacing free of charge. This service is provided up to three (3) times per board after date of purchase and is recommended yearly. Some added charges may apply to custom boards. Shipping not included.
